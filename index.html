TEAL TOGETHER FESTIVAL


DECEMBER 8-10, 2024

DAY 1 - DECEMBER 8

5K Bubble Run or Walk
Where: SF Great Highway
Time: 9AM - 12PM

The festival starts with an energetic kickoff event along the scenic Great Highway in San Francisco. Participants can choose to run or walk in the 5K route while being surrounded by colorful bubbles! Creating a joyful and lively atmosphere.

DAY 2 - DECEMBER 9

Lunch and Learn, Field Day, and Music
Where: The Fort Mason Center
Time: 11AM - 4PM

Day 2 starts with a 2 hour "Lunch and Learn" session beginning at 11AM at The Fort Mason Center. OCRA will facilitate a Q&A discussing ovarian cancer. Later, it's a lively field day with games and music! Tickets support ovarian cancer research, with games raising awareness and live music adding to the fun.

DAY 3 - DECEMBER 10

Zumba and Women-Owned Vendor Market
Where: The Fort Mason Center
Time: 10AM - 4PM

Day 3 starts with a high-energy 1 hour Zumba class beginning at 11AM. This session promotes physical activity and unity. At 12PM to 4PM, the festival concludes with a market featuring local women-owned vendors. Attendees can browse jewelry, clothes, art, and more while supporting female entrepreneurs.
