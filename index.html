TEAL TOGETHER FESTIVAL
DECEMBER 8-10 2024

DAY 1 - DECEMBER 8

5K Bubble Run/Walk

The festival starts with an energetic kickoff event along the scenic Great Highway in San Francisco. Participants can choose to run or walk in the 5K route while being surrounded by the colorful bubbles, creating a joyful and liely atmosphere.

DAY 2 - DECEMBER 9

Lunch and Learn, Field Day, and Music

Day two starts with a "Lunch and Learn" session at The Fort Mason Center, discussing ovarian cancer. OCRA facilitates a Q&A. Later, it's a lively field day with games and music. Tickets support ovarian cancer research, with games raising awareness and live music adding to the fun.

DAY 3 - DECEMBER 10

Zumba and Women-Owned Vendor Market

Day three starts with a high-energy Zumba class, open to all attendees. This session promotes physical activity and unity. Later, the festival concludes with a market featuring local women-owned vendors. Attendees can browse jewelry, clothes, art, and more while supporting female entrepreneurs. This market, supported by OCRA, offers unique items and contributes to the cause.
